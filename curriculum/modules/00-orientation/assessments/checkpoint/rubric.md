# Module 00 Checkpoint Rubric

| Category | Weight | Meets standard |
|---|---:|---|
| Repository navigation | 20% | Correctly explains `metadata/`, `curriculum/`, `tools/`, `docs/`, and `dist/`. |
| Zero-magic understanding | 20% | Explains no-hidden-assumption learning and gives a concrete example. |
| Workflow understanding | 20% | Distinguishes lessons, labs, projects, assessments, starters, and solutions. |
| Running/debugging readiness | 20% | Explains `go run`, `go test`, and writes a high-signal debugging question. |
| Roadmap/portfolio planning | 20% | Gives a realistic next-step plan and identifies portfolio evidence. |

## Passing standard

Pass: 80% or higher with no zero in any category.

Retake: any category below 60% should be revised after reviewing the relevant lesson.

## Feedback guide

When reviewing your own answers, ask:

- Did I explain using my own words?
- Did I give evidence, not slogans?
- Did I connect file paths to curriculum behavior?
- Did I show how I would use this in the next module?
